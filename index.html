The Problem

Most retail investors focus on returns but do not truly understand risk. Existing tools are often too technical, too fragmented, or too passive to help users make better decisions in real time.

Risk is hard to understand

Retail investors often do not know the downside of a trade, the real risk-reward tradeoff, or whether a position fits their financial situation.

Tools are too complex

Most risk metrics are built for professionals, not for everyday users who need simple, direct, and actionable guidance.

Finance and investing are disconnected

Most platforms ignore spending behavior, monthly cash flow, and personal financial pressure when evaluating investment risk.

Our Solution

RiskPilot gives retail investors a simple risk intelligence layer. Instead of only looking at market data, we also incorporate portfolio structure, spending patterns, and personal cash flow to produce clearer and more personalized risk guidance.

Simple for users

We translate complex risk analytics into clear explanations, alerts, and suggested actions that non-professional investors can actually use.

Built around specialized AI

Our platform uses specially trained, risk-control-centered AI bots designed to focus on disciplined decision support rather than hype-driven trading suggestions.

How It Works

1. Input Your Financial Data

Users input portfolio holdings, income, expenses, and spending records to build a complete risk profile.

2. Receive AI Risk Interpretation

Our AI bots analyze the risk context of each trade and explain it in clear language centered on risk control.

3. Act With Better Timing

Users receive personalized alerts and timing guidance based on changes in portfolio exposure, cash flow, and market conditions.
